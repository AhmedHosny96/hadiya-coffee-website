Our Story

With the aspiration Ethiopian being the center of Arabica coffee has initiated the founder to become part of the Ethiopian coffee industry thus created a momentum for the establishment of Hadiya coffee company in September 2021. we provide 100% arabica premium value added coffee products for the consumer, market, hotels restaurant and cafe sector, our coffee which is Harar coffee is known for its distinctive flavor and taste. Our standard packaging includes 500gm and 1 KG. We will strive to provide you with a premium and new coffee products as we expand knowledge in coffee. A purpose-built roastery was designed and built, following a detailed assessment and research into the coffee business in Ethiopia and on the continent.
